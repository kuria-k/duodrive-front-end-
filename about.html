OUR STORY

Born from pure passion in November 2024, Duo Drive was founded by two spirited car enthusiasts who shared a vision: to revolutionize mobility in Kenya by blending performance, style, and innovation. What started as a garage dream soon evolved into a dynamic automotive hub offering unique car rentals, expert vehicle sourcing, and driver services tailored for thrill-seekers and everyday commuters alike. At Duo Drive, every ride tells a story—and yours is just getting started.

MISSION

To redefine urban mobility in Kenya by providing stylish, reliable, and affordable automotive solutions tailored for passionate drivers and everyday explorers alike.

VISSION

To become Kenya’s leading automotive lifestyle brand—where innovation meets experience, and every journey sparks joy.
